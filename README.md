Ever dropped your sword in the middle of a fight?
Or accidentally thrown a shulker box straight into lava?

This mod protects you from costly mistakes like that!

It disables the drop key for items in your hotbar, preventing accidental drops during normal gameplay. You can still drop items intentionally by opening any GUI (inventory, chest, crafting table, etc.), so you stay in full control when you actually mean to throw something away.

**Commands:**

* `/dropprevent on` – Enable hotbar drop protection
* `/dropprevent off` – Disable hotbar drop protection
